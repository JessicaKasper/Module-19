Jessica Kasper

The Friendly UX/UI Designer

Jessica has always had a passion for technology. Beginning a streaming career at 15, she traveled across the country participating in video game charity marathons and building connections in the tech world. She then taught herself to build computers and studied hardware in her free time, always on deck if a family member needed computer help. She transformed her whole life moving from Florida to Colorado in pursuit of a tech job, where she found a passion for UX/UI. She's incredibly excited to see where she can go in this new career path.
